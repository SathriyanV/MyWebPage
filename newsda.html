NewsDA

NewsDA is a news application which provides news information from all over the world. All news presented in this application is from worlds top most credible sources.

Download pdf

How it works

Home

Home section presents you the top Indian headlines of the day with 7 different categories to choose from.

Sub Categories:

  • Business
  • Entertainment
  • Health
  • Science
  • Sports
  • Technology
  • General

Discover

Discover section lets you search news on any topic and also provides option to read news from top sources.

Bookmarks

Bookmark section helps you to save news offline to read later. It has also has delete option to delete the news.

Settings

Settings section provides basic details about the app and developer along with one customisation option.

Technology Used

  • Swift
  • SwiftUI
  • RestAPI
  • Core Data
  • JSON
  • Github
  • MVVM
